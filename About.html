About Us

Fancy food makes our day! Does it make yours too? Food is the thing that keeps us happy even on the gloomiest of days, We are Mumbai based foodbloggers,we give review about the places we visit and the food we eat! We call ourself tieghan gerard of mumbai(borivali).Never the less we dream to be indias best foodblogger but our main goal is to visit and have all the best cuisines around country.We prefer local and street food rather than fancy restaurants.
